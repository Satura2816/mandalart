🌸 About the Artist

“Each dot, a thought. Each line, a breath. Each mandala, a universe of peace.”

Hi! I’m Sneha V, but in the world of creation and calm, I’m simply the soul behind Mandala Artistry.

Based in Kerala, India, I find joy in turning quiet moments into intricate mandala designs that speak to the heart. What began as a hobby transformed into a meditative journey—a way to express inner calm through geometry and grace.
